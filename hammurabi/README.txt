BUILD YOUR CIVILIZATION

A game in which you decide how to lead your little colonisation to a prosperous civilization.

Decisions you can make during a turn:
- buy/sell acres of land
    - you won't be able to sell more acres than you have
    - you won't be able to buy more acres than grains allow you
- feed your population a number of grains
    - you won't be able to feed the population more grains than you have
- plant acres of land
    - you won't be able to plant more acres than you have
